# PROP A — On-body notes (cipher + key hint)

These are the two scraps carried by the defenders. Split them across two bodies so no single
search yields the whole hint. Hand-write them on torn/aged paper for immersion.

> Sanitized template. Replace `{{...}}` with your own values. The point of these notes is to
> tell players *what* cipher and *which* key, **without** giving the key value itself — that
> lives in PROP B.

### Design notes (remove before printing)

- Keep these two **physically separate**. The whole "distributed key assembly" mechanic relies
  on no one prop being self-sufficient.
- The key hint must point at something — here, the literal string "operative code", whose value
  is buried in PROP B's technical table.
- A team that finds only PROP A knows the method but cannot decrypt. That gap is the puzzle.
